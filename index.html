Channelizing youth for nation building towards health for all.

A Step
Towards Nation Building

Youth summit is a annual date to remember. NSS IITP in collaboration with Bihar State AIDS Control Society is going to uplift this year's summit on 21-22nd March above all the spheres. The theme evolves around "Channelizing youth for nation building Towards health for all". This day long fest includes talks, music, dance and a number of events. It will be a space to disrupt old ways, the intention is to create a space for collaboration, sharing, celebration and collective narrative building. It is going to be a gathering to strengthen our youth leadership. Finally the Youth fest intends to be a space for innovation, problem solving to deep dive in their social change journey, build shared understanding of our challenges and create collective vision for our future along other young people.We warmly welcome the Nodal officers of Red Ribbon club and program coordinators of NSS along with the volunteers.
